⏰ Digital Clock

Real-time display across multiple timezones

ℹ️ How to Use

• Add Custom Timezone: Click the "+ Add Custom Timezone" button to add timezones not in the default list.

• Toggle Format: Click "Toggle Format" to switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time display.

• Reset: Click "Reset to Default" to restore the default timezone list.

• UTC Offset: Positive values (+) are east of UTC, negative (-) are west of UTC.
